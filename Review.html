Favorite Food and Restaurants

Gyuu Niku (牛肉) - Taipei

A must-visit spot in Taipei for beef lovers! Famous for its premium cuts, rich flavors, and perfectly grilled meats.

Review: "The best beef I've had in Taipei! Perfectly grilled and melts in your mouth." ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Shishlik Pita x Kebab - Taipei

A hidden gem in Taipei serving authentic Middle Eastern flavors! Known for its juicy kebabs, fresh pita, and bold spices.

Must try: Grilled lamb skewers, beef kebabs, and handmade pita. ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
