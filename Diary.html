Mini Blog

Updates about what I have been up to in past days

Yingge Ceramics Museum

Published on January 25, 2025

Today, I visited the ceramics museum in the Yingge district and found this amazing ceramic art called Bearded Dragon’s Habitat by Melis Buyruk (2022). The design was mesmerizing and reminded me of my dream pet!

Unexpected Keelung Trip

Published on February 28, 2025

We were supposed to go ice skating in Nangang, but at the last minute, we changed our destination to Keelung. Unexpectedly, it turned out to be an amazing trip with great weather, delicious food, and good company!

Unexpected Food Encounter

Published on March 2, 2025

A friend visiting from Singapore led us to a cozy Middle Eastern restaurant in Zhongzheng. The potato wedges were surprisingly the best I’ve had in Taiwan, and the Shakshouka paired beautifully with the hummus and kebab!

Exploring the Glass House

Published on March 20, 2025

The weather was too nice to stay indoors, so I explored Taipei on my own. I stumbled upon a beautiful botanical garden with an air-conditioned glass house—such a perfect escape on a sunny day!
